How can I help?

Although it is unavoidable for electronic equipment to eventually become redundant, you can follow the tips available on the left slide show to minimize the impact your home or office e-waste has on the environment. Most types of e-waste contain many valuable resources that can be recovered and reused. Often much less energy is needed to recover these resources than to produce new materials.

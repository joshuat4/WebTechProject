Collection Partners

Want to make a difference? Join our program to become a collection partner. If you own a business, you can volunteer to host an E-Waste recycling unit.
By joining our program,

  • You help your business do its part in the fight against climate change
  • You raise awareness for the rapidly growing problem of E-Waste.
  • You make it easier and convenient for your customers and community deal responsibly with their E-Waste.

How the program works

ReeTech recognizes the need for clear and simple guidance when working with participating collection sites. The program provides a variety of support materials and has a team ready to provide assistance to you every step along the way. ReeTech acts as the middleman in facilitating the entire recycling process, from on-boarding, to collection, all the way through to the disposal of the collected electronic waste.

To join the collection program, click on the button below and sign up using the form that follows. Alternatively just contact us and a ReeTech member will guide you through each step.
